Chapter 1: Physical Quantities

Lesson Summary:

Chapter 1 introduces the basic concept of physical quantities and measurement in physics. Physical quantities are properties of matter that can be measured, such as length, mass, time, temperature, and electric current. Each quantity has a standard unit defined by the International System of Units (SI). Proper measurement is essential for experiments and scientific calculations.

Key Points:

  • Definition of Physical Quantities
  • Base and Derived Units
  • SI Units for length, mass, time, temperature, current
  • Accuracy and Precision in Measurement
  • Measurement Instruments and Techniques
